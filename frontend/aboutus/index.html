Welcome

Welcome to Spotted! Submit your photos! Complete achievements! Race to the top of the leaderboards! All while supporting wildlife in your local area!

How to Play!

The game is simple, snap a photo of a vertebrate animal in the wild, in the UK and submit it for verification by our in-house AI bot. Provide your user details and location of your sighting and you shall be awarded XP points based on both rarity and frequency of species sighted.

Achievements and Unlockables

Complete achievements, present in the My Account page, to unlock epic titles which you can customise your profile with, as well as bonus XP to shoot you up on the leaderboards.

Profile pictures can also be unlocked by sighting whole families of species present around the UK. Examples of families being: deers, rabbits and hares and falcons. Show off your hard-earned rewards on the monthly and all time leaderboards which highlight the hardest working spotters in the country.

The Map

See community sightings of species around the country. It allows us to celebrate UK wildlife, whilst eluding you to species in your area which you can seek out to get your score up. You can filter findings by animal group as well as rarity!
